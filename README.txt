# YoungBuckNZ Portfolio

Welcome to the official portfolio site of **Hector McNeilly**, the creative force behind **YoungBuckNZ**. This site showcases my photography, filmmaking, and multimedia storytelling — built for adventurers, brands, and projects that thrive outdoors.

## 🌄 About

I’m an aspiring filmmaker and content creator from New Zealand. My passion lies in capturing authentic, wild, and cinematic moments — whether it’s in the mountains, at sea, or deep in the bush. This site acts as both my personal portfolio and a creative hub for connecting with clients and collaborators.

## 📁 Project Structure

- `index.html` – Main landing page
- `style.css` – Custom styling for layout and typography
- `images/` – Gallery and background visuals
- `videos/` – Embedded video content (linked from YouTube)
- `about.html`, `services.html` – Supporting pages (if applicable)
